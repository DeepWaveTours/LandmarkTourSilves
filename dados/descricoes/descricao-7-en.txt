The Mercado Municipal da Avenida, also known as the Municipal Market or Avenida Market, is a public building in the center of the city of Lagos, Portugal.

Description

The building is divided into three floors: the ground floor is occupied by fish and seafood stalls, butcher shops, and an ice shop; the first floor houses a café and six shops selling various products, especially fruits and vegetables; the third floor features a restaurant and a terrace with views over the bay of Lagos, as well as a pedestrian access to the Centro Ciência Viva de Lagos, located at the rear of the Municipal Market. Communication between the three floors is facilitated by a staircase and a panoramic elevator.
